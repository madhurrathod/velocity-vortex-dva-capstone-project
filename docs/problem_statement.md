# Problem Statement

The bank is investing in direct marketing campaigns to sell term deposits, but overall conversion is low. The project aims to identify which customer profiles, outreach channels, prior-campaign outcomes, and contact frequencies are linked to higher subscription rates so the bank can target prospects more effectively and improve campaign efficiency.

## Main Business Questions

1. Which customer segments show the highest term-deposit subscription rates?
2. Which campaign attributes are associated with better outcomes?
3. Does repeated follow-up improve conversions or reduce efficiency?
4. How should the bank prioritize channels and segments in future campaigns?
